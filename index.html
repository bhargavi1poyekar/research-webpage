Intelligent Flappy Bird Player

Explored the use of reinforcement learning (RL) algorithms to train an intelligent Flappy Bird player in navigating complex mazes of pipes. The study compared Q-learning, Deep Q-Networks (DQN), and Policy Gradient, highlighting DQN as the most promising with superior performance, achieving a maximum reward of 14 over minimal iterations. These insights offer valuable perspectives on the efficacy of various RL techniques in dynamic gaming environments.
